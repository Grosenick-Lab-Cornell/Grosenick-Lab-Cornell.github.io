Better models need better measurements. We build and apply **neurotechnology** to
read and perturb brain activity: TMS combined with high-density EEG in patients
undergoing accelerated stimulation for treatment-resistant depression, and
closed-loop approaches that turn those signals into individualized, adaptive
interventions.
